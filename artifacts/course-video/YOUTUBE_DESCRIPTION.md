Constructive Critical Reasoning — AI-Powered Course

A complete, self-paced course that trains one demanding skill: committing to the richest, most-falsifiable conclusion a body of evidence actually supports — and naming the cleanest test that could prove you wrong. No math, no coding, no technical background required.

This is not another "find reasons to doubt" exercise. Constructive Critical Reasoning inverts the usual instinct. Hedging is the failure mode. The boldest, best-supported model earns top credit; the cautious "you can't really conclude anything" dodge earns almost nothing; padding that binds no data scores low; and a confident claim the data actually defeats earns zero.

A DEEP, AI-POWERED LEARNING SYSTEM

• Three-depth lessons — read every lesson at Short, Medium, or Long length, rewritten by AI while preserving the same examples and objectives.
• Section-scoped AI tutor — highlight any passage and get an answer that streams back in real time, grounded in the exact section you're reading, with suggested starter questions for each lecture.
• One homework per section, your format — choose multiple choice, a hybrid of multiple choice plus short written, or fully written. You get a single attempt, and it locks the moment you submit.
• Inverted partial-credit grading — answers are scored on how much evidence they bind and how falsifiable a test they commit to, with a written rationale returned on every item.
• Two-layer AI-authorship detection — every submission is screened by both a text classifier and a keystroke-pattern analysis, each with a human-readable explanation.
• Adaptive practice — generated scenario problems that get harder after a streak and easier after a miss, with an explanation on every answer.
• Four-phase diagnostic assessments — the same reasoning instrument runs at four checkpoints across the course so you can see your reasoning grow.
• Live analytics — track attempts, accuracy, streaks, and per-section mastery, plus a recent-activity feed.
